Common questions

Do you do fixed price?

Yes — for defined scope. Larger projects use milestones or time-based billing once scope is clear.

Can I start small?

Absolutely. Micro-engagements are designed to roll into bigger builds if the need grows.

What's required to begin?

A deposit, access to your accounts, brand assets (if you have them), and a clear goal.

Do you do design only?

Yes. UX structure, wireframes, and full UI kits are all available as standalone deliverables.

Do you work with existing sites?

Yes. Audits, redesigns, and migrations are all in scope. We'll assess before we quote.

Do you provide hosting?

We can manage it for you, but you own the account. We don't mark up hosting costs.

What about content?

You provide it by default. Copywriting is available as an add-on if you need it.
