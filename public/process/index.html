How we work.

Six steps. No guesswork, no disappearing acts. You know exactly where things stand at every stage.

01

Discovery

We sit down - or hop on a call - and learn your business. What you do, who you serve, what's broken, what you want to build. No assumptions.

  • Business & audience analysis
  • Goals & success metrics
  • Competitive & market review
  • Scope & feasibility check
02

Strategy & Architecture

Before any pixels or code, we map the structure. Sitemap, user flows, information hierarchy - the blueprint that everything else is built on.

  • Sitemap & page structure
  • User-flow planning
  • Technical stack selection
  • SEO & performance strategy
03

Design

Wireframes to final mockups. We design for how people actually use the site - not just how it looks in a portfolio screenshot. You review and give feedback at each stage.

  • Wireframes & layout
  • Visual design & brand system
  • Responsive breakpoints
  • Feedback & iteration rounds
04

Development

Clean code, built to last. We build the front-end, wire up any back-end logic, integrate your CMS, and set up the infrastructure - all on a staging environment you can review in real time.

  • Front-end build
  • Back-end & integrations
  • CMS setup & content
  • Staging environment access
05

Testing & Launch

Nothing goes live until it's right. Cross-browser, cross-device, performance benchmarked. DNS configured, SSL locked, redirects tested. Then we flip the switch.

  • Cross-browser & device QA
  • Performance & speed audit
  • DNS, SSL & redirect setup
  • Go-live & monitoring
06

Support & Iteration

Launch isn't the end - it's the start. We monitor, update, and keep improving based on real data. You're never left to figure it out alone.

  • Uptime & performance monitoring
  • Updates & security patches
  • Analytics & reporting
  • Feature iteration & retainer support

How long does it take?

Depends on scope. A focused landing site: 2-4 weeks. A full web app: 6-12 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline in Discovery - not a guess.

How much input do I need to give?

You're involved at Discovery and Design. After that, we handle the rest. We don't ask questions we can figure out ourselves.

What if I change my mind mid-project?

It happens. We scope changes honestly - if it's small, we absorb it. If it's a shift in direction, we'll talk through the impact before anything moves.

Ready to get started?

Tell us about your project. We'll figure out the rest together.
